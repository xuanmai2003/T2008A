GROUP RATES 10+

group reservations

At Ripley’s Aquarium of Canada we offer reduced rates for groups of 10 people or more.
Reservations are required. Groups are recommended to book a week prior to the scheduled arrival date. However, depending on availability, last-minute requests may be accommodated.
Please note, your reservation is not confirmed until we have provided a formal invoice.

request a reservation

Please download the reservation form for your group size/type and email it to Groups@RipleysAquariumofCanada.com.
A Group Sales Coordinator will confirm receipt of your reservation form and provide a formal invoice within 2-3 business days.
Full payment is required with final numbers three days before scheduled visit.
