DRIVING DIRECTIONS

EAST OF TORONTO?

  • Take Highway 401 (MacDonald Cartier Freeway) to the Don Valley Parkway
  • Go south on the D.V.P. to the Gardiner Expressway
  • Go west on the Gardiner to the Spadina Avenue exit
  • Exit on Spadina traveling north and turn right (east) on Bremner Boulevard

WEST OF TORONTO?

  • Take Highway 401 (MacDonald Cartier Freeway) to Highway 427
  • Go south on the 427 to the Gardiner Expressway
  • Go east on the Gardiner to the Spadina Avenue exit
  • Exit on Spadina traveling north and turn right (east) on Bremner Boulevard or
  • Take the Queen Elizabeth Way to the Gardiner Expressway
  • Continue traveling east on the Gardiner to the Spadina Avenue exit
  • Exit on Spadina traveling north and turn right (east) on Bremner Boulevard

NORTH OF TORONTO?

  • Take Highway 404 until it becomes the Don Valley Parkway
  • Continue south on the Don Valley Parkway to the Gardiner Expressway
  • Go west on the Gardiner to the Spadina Avenue exit
  • Exit on Spadina traveling north and turn right (east) on Bremner Boulevard

PUBLIC TRANSIT

TTC SUBWAY

  • From Union Station: Follow the SkyWalk west or walk west on the south side of Front Street to York Street or Simcoe Street. Follow York Street or Simcoe Street south to Bremner Blvd. Walk west on Bremner Blvd.
  • From St. Andrews Station: Walk west on King Street to John Street and follow John Street south, across the John Street bridge to Bremner Blvd.

TTC STREETCAR

  • From the King Street route: Get off at John Street and walk south to Bremner Blvd.
  • From the Spadina Avenue route: Get off at Bremner Boulevard and walk east
  • From the LRT (Light Rail Transit) line: Get off at Rees Street and walk north to Bremner Blvd.

TRAIN/BUS

    Check with the Toronto Transit Commission for details on bus route information: (416)-393-INFO.
  • From Union Station: Walk west on the south side of Front Street
  • Visit www.gotransit.com for train schedules and more information

DROP OFF

Tour and school buses can drop off passengers in front of the Ripley’s Aquarium, on the North side of Bremner Blvd.
*Please ask your Group Sales Representative when making arrangements for your group.
