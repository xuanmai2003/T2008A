REQUESTING YOUR EVENT
Invite your guests to immerse themselves in an incredible underwater experience at one of Toronto’s unique event destinations. With nine galleries to explore, Ripley’s Aquarium of Canada provides a truly one of kind event experience for corporate clients, galas, celebrations, fundraisers, holiday parties, meetings and more!

catering

The Aquarium’s carefully selected list of approved catering and supply partners will work with you and the Events team to ensure a seamless planning experience and impeccable event. View our complete list of catering partners below!
Your chosen suppliers will guide you through the planning process, from creating a customized menu, to creating the perfect event layout and décor design, and executing a flawless event on the big day.

Unique Experiences

In addition to offering a unique space, the Aquarium Events team can offer a variety of unique experiences to enhance your event, from Behind the Scenes tours to custom Dive Shows.
For more information on requesting your event, please contact a representative from our Events team at events@ripleysaquariumofcanada.com or call 647-351-FISH (3474) Ext. 2652
